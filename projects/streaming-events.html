Streaming Events Pipeline

Context: Ingest and process high-volume events with low latency and strong observability.

Design: Kafka → Spark Structured Streaming → Delta/Iceberg lakehouse → dbt marts for analytics.

  • Exactly-once semantics with checkpointing and idempotent sinks.
  • Prometheus/Grafana dashboards for lag, throughput, error rate.
  • Cost controls with compaction, retention, and tiered storage.

Tech: Kafka, Spark Streaming, Delta Lake, dbt, Grafana/Prometheus.

Role: Data Engineer — streaming jobs, lakehouse tables, and monitoring.
