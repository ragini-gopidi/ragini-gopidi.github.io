About Me

I’m a Data Engineer with experience designing and building end-to-end ETL pipelines and cloud data platforms, primarily on Azure. I focus on reliable ingestion, scalable transformation with Spark/Python, clean data models, and the observability that keeps pipelines trustworthy. I’ve delivered projects in healthcare and finance, collaborating closely with analytics and product teams to ship data that drives decisions.

My toolkit includes Python, SQL, PySpark, Azure Data Factory, Synapse, Databricks, Airflow, dbt, Kafka, and Snowflake/Redshift/BigQuery. I care about pragmatic architecture, data contracts, and quality gates so stakeholders can trust what they see. I’m aiming for a Senior Data Engineer role where I can own systems end-to-end, mentor teammates, and continue leveling up the platform.

Outside of work, I enjoy traveling and exploring new places.
